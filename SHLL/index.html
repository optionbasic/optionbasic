Why Is Everyone Talking About Tortoise Acquisition(NYSE:SHLL) And Hylion Holdings?

Tortoise acquisition(SHLL) shares rose 300% when they announced a merger with the private company Hyliion Holdings. The new company is hoping to become a major player in an $800 billion market. Hyliion claims that their hybrid drive systems significantly lower the cost of heavy truck ownership through fuel efficiency and regulatory compliance. Hyliion, like the surging EV makers Nikola and Tesla, is seeking to disrupt the heavy-duty truck business- but not by building the entire truck. Instead, Hyliion wants to sell its powertrain solutions to existing truck makers. Their advantages include having industry leading partners from the transportation and logistics sectors, and a high customer demand for the product. Unlike Tesla and Nikola, their systems are compatible with any class 8 truck, from any manufacturer- making them a practical solution to the problem.
